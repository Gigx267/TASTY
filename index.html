We are Pocho

A unique and vibrant dining experience that combines the flavors of Africa with contemporary flair. Our menu showcases a fusion of traditional African dishes with a modern twist, reflecting the diverse cultures and cuisines of the continent. Our commitment to using fresh, locally sourced ingredients allows us to create bold and delicious flavors that truly embody the spirit of Africa.

At Pocho , we strive to create an atmosphere that is both inviting and fun. Our space is decorated with vibrant colors and unique art pieces, reflecting the energy and diversity of Africa. Whether you're enjoying a leisurely brunch with friends or grabbing a quick bite to eat, our welcoming and friendly staff will make sure you feel at home

Pocho's Menu

Our menu features a range of dishes that showcase the richness and diversity of African cuisine. From juicy grilled meats to flavorful stews and curries, we have something for everyone. Whether you're in the mood for a light and healthy meal or something hearty and filling, our menu is designed to satisfy your cravings. We use only the freshest and locally sourced ingredients to create our dishes, ensuring that every bite is bursting with flavor. Some of our most popular items include our spicy Jollof Rice, slow-cooked Egusi Soup, and our succulent Suya skewers.

Our Unique Theme

The name "Pocho" is a popular stew native to the Tiv people of the Benue Valley. This delicious stew, often paired with various other soups, symbolizes togetherness. Coming together to enjoy great food and drinks is the spirt of our Restaurant
