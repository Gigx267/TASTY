About Us

Pocho

is a unique dining experience that combines the rich cultural heritage of Africa with contemporary flavors and ingredients. Our menu features a diverse selection of dishes inspired by the diverse cuisines found throughout the African continent. Our aim is to provide a space where people can come together and enjoy delicious food, live music and a lively atmosphere, while learning about the cultural heritage of Africa

Our chefs use only the freshest ingredients to create dishes that are bursting with flavor, and we take great pride in sourcing locally grown produce whenever possible. Our menu is designed to cater to a wide range of tastes and dietary requirements, including vegan, vegetarian and gluten-free options. In addition to our food, we also offer a carefully curated selection of African-inspired drinks, including smoothies, teas, and locally sourced coffee. Our dining room is decorated with traditional African art and textiles, creating a warm and inviting atmosphere that will transport you to the heart of Africa. We believe in giving back to the community and supporting local initiatives, and we are dedicated to creating a positive impact in the areas where we operate. Our team is passionate about the food, drinks, and culture we serve, and we are committed to providing an exceptional dining experience for all of our guests.
